Welcome to PDFMerge Pro. Whether you need to combine legal contracts, gather monthly invoices into an archive, or stitch together school assignments with standardized page sizes, our tool provides a professional, instant solution directly in your web browser.

Advanced Page Standardization

Combining PDFs from different sources usually results in a messy document with pages of varying shapes and sizes. Our advanced WebAssembly engine solves this by allowing you to Force Page Sizes (A4 or US Letter) and Force Orientation (Portrait or Landscape). The system will intelligently scale and embed your original pages into the new standardized formats, ensuring a clean, professional output.

Why Choose a Client-Side PDF Merger?

Standard online PDF mergers require you to upload your highly sensitive documents to a remote server. This creates massive data privacy risks. Our tool is engineered differently.

  • Total Data Privacy: Your documents never leave your computer's RAM. We cannot view, store, or access your files.
  • Zero Upload Times: Because there are no servers involved, merging ten massive 50MB PDF files happens almost instantly.
