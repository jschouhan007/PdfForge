The Ultimate Free PDF Splitter & Organizer

Welcome to PDFSplit Pro. Whether you need to extract a single chapter from a massive eBook, separate double-sided scans into odd and even files, or remove a confidential page before emailing a contract, our tool provides a professional, instant solution directly in your web browser.

Advanced PDF Splitting Modes

We've engineered 5 unique splitting algorithms to handle any document scenario:

  • Extract Specific Pages: Enter a custom range (e.g., 1-5, 8, 12) to pull only the exact pages you need into a fresh PDF document.
  • Remove Pages: The opposite of extraction. List the pages you want gone, and we will generate a clean PDF with those pages permanently deleted.
  • Odd/Even Separation: Perfect for duplex scanning workflows. Instantly divide a document into two separate files—one containing odd pages, the other even.
  • Fixed Chunks: Break a 100-page file into ten 10-page documents automatically.

Why Choose a Client-Side Tool?

Standard online PDF editors force you to upload your sensitive tax returns and legal contracts to a remote cloud server. This exposes you to tracking, data harvesting, and privacy breaches. Our tool runs 100% inside your device. Your files never touch the internet, ensuring absolute privacy and lightning-fast processing speeds regardless of your connection.

Premium Formatting Features

Supported by our ad network, you can access powerful document formatting tools for free. Reorient incorrectly scanned pages using our Page Rotation Tool, or use Form Flattening to lock fillable PDF fields into read-only text so they can't be altered by the recipient.
