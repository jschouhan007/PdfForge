The Ultimate Free Custom QR Code Generator

Welcome to QRGen Pro, the most secure and versatile tool to generate high-resolution QR codes directly in your browser. Whether you are a business owner looking to create a custom QR code with a center logo, or an individual wanting a quick Wi-Fi login QR code for guests, our tool handles it all instantly.

Why Use Our Client-Side QR Generator?

Unlike other QR code makers that upload your data to external servers, our generator uses 100% client-side rendering. This means your sensitive website links, personal vCard contact details, and private Wi-Fi passwords never leave your device. It is mathematically impossible for your data to be intercepted.

  • Lightning Fast: No server lag or waiting in queues.
  • Infinite Customization: Adjust foreground and background colors, change the dot patterns, and apply sleek gradients.
  • Vector Downloads: Export your final design as a high-quality PNG or a scalable SVG for professional printing on business cards and billboards.

Types of QR Codes You Can Create

Our dynamic interface allows you to switch between multiple data formats seamlessly:

  • Website URLs: Perfect for marketing materials, restaurant menus, and social media links.
  • Plain Text: Encode serial numbers, secret messages, or inventory tracking data.
  • Wi-Fi Network: Generate a code that instantly connects any scanning smartphone to your home or office router without typing a password.
  • vCard (Digital Business Card): Share your first name, last name, phone number, and email instantly. When scanned, it prompts the user to save you directly to their contacts app.
