The Most Secure Free Excel to PDF Converter

Welcome to Excel2PDF Pro. Formatting complex spreadsheets into clean, printable PDFs can be a nightmare. Our advanced WebAssembly tool reads your raw spreadsheet data and renders it locally into beautiful, paginated PDF reports without ever sending your sensitive financial data to a cloud server.

Advanced Spreadsheet Formatting

We've engineered intelligent parsing to handle your data perfectly:

  • Auto-Fit Scaling: No more cut-off columns. Select 'Fit to Page Width' and our engine will automatically calculate the optimal font size and cell width to ensure your entire data table fits neatly on the page.
  • Multi-Format Support: Natively accepts .xls, .xlsx, and .csv files seamlessly via the drag-and-drop zone.
  • Orientation Control: Wide tables? Force the PDF engine to generate Landscape documents.
